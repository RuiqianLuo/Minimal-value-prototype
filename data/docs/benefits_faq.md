# Benefits FAQ

Acme Cloud Solutions provides health, dental, and vision coverage starting on the first day of the month after an employee's start date. Employees must submit benefit elections within 14 days of receiving their enrollment email.

The company offers a 1500 USD annual learning and development stipend for courses, conferences, books, and professional memberships related to the employee's role. Manager approval is required before purchase.

Parents can take 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Employees who adopt a child are eligible for the same paid leave benefit.

Employees may request one-time home office equipment reimbursement up to 500 USD during their first year of employment.
