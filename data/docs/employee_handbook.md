# Employee Handbook

Acme Cloud Solutions uses a hybrid-first work model. New hires can work remotely up to three days per week during their first 90 days if their manager confirms onboarding milestones are on track. After 90 days, teams can choose fully remote, hybrid, or office-based schedules with director approval.

The standard working window is 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time for collaboration. Outside that window, employees may flex their schedules as long as they communicate availability in Slack and the HR system.

Paid time off accrues at 1.5 days per month for full-time employees. Employees can carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year. Additional unused days expire on January 31 unless an exception is approved by HR for medical or parental leave.

Managers must complete a 30-day and 60-day onboarding check-in for each new hire and confirm role clarity, access setup, and training completion.
