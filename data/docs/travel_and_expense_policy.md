# Travel And Expense Policy

Employees can book domestic travel directly if the total estimated trip cost is below 1200 USD and the travel is included in an approved quarterly budget. Trips above 1200 USD require manager approval before booking.

Any international travel requires both manager approval and finance approval, regardless of cost.

Meals are reimbursable up to 70 USD per travel day in the United States and 90 USD per travel day internationally. Alcohol is not reimbursable.

Expense reports must be submitted within 10 calendar days after trip completion. Receipts are required for any single expense above 25 USD.
