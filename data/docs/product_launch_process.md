# Product Launch Process

Every customer-facing launch requires a launch brief, support readiness plan, analytics plan, and rollback owner. The product manager owns the launch brief and success metrics, while engineering owns rollback planning and technical monitoring.

For medium or high-risk launches, teams must schedule a cross-functional readiness review at least five business days before launch. Required attendees are product, engineering, support, marketing, and security when customer data is affected.

Launch success is measured during the first two weeks using activation, support ticket volume, defect rate, and qualitative customer feedback.

If a launch is rolled back, the owning PM must publish a short retrospective within three business days with the decision trigger, customer impact, and follow-up actions.
