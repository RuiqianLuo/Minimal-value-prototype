# User Personas

## Persona 1: Maya, Operations Manager

- Needs fast answers to policy and process questions
- Often answers repeat questions from new team members
- Values confidence, clarity, and source visibility
- Pain point: too much time spent digging through docs or Slack history

## Persona 2: Leo, New Employee

- Needs help understanding benefits, onboarding, and internal rules
- May not know which team owns each topic
- Values speed and self-service
- Pain point: asking "basic" questions can feel slow or awkward

## Persona 3: Nina, AI Product Analyst

- Evaluates whether the assistant is accurate enough for internal rollout
- Wants measurable outputs, failure cases, and a clear MVP path
- Values groundedness, evaluation design, and interpretable logs
- Pain point: many demos look impressive but are hard to trust or measure
